I I N N D D E E X X

The Network

As a member of Iinnddeexx, you will be a part of a (hopefully soon-to-be) large and fast-growing network of creatives and makers, a sort of virtual directory that can help put you in touch with other people who do what you do.

The Member Card

The Iinnddeexx badge is something that you can quickly and easily add to your website to declare your membership. Think of it kind of like a union card; a way to show people that you value the power of numbers.

The Site

Your name, website, and information will be listed on our site, which acts as a tool for discovering new people in your field, making connections, and finding collaborators.
